Giants Organization

Format of the Giants Organization

The Giants organization consists of five teams ordered in level of skill. In descending order, the SF Giants affiliated teams are: The San Francisco Giants, The Sacramento River Cats, The Richmond Flying Squirrels, The Eugene Emeralds, and the San Jose Giants. Among these teams are players who can rise up the ranks based upon their success. On this page I would like to highlight two different standout players in the Giants organization.
